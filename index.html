01 · About

From rules and concepts to daily execution

This program is designed to help teams turn Pricing & Commercial Policy into concrete business decisions. The focus is practical: you will work with your country data and cases, apply pricing logic through exercises, and define actions that can be used after the training.

✓

Understand key pricing concepts

Refresh the key pricing logic and connect it to SE ways of working.

↗

Apply to country cases

Work with your country data, practical cases and actions that can be used in business reviews.

∞

Build execution consistency

Exchange with peers and strengthen alignment across countries and roles.

04 · Modules

Training snapshot

Module 1

Price Strategy

How to choose the right pricing battle by basket for your market and translate it into actions.

Concepts / tools
Pricing Strategy MatrixPricing market / basketPricing powerPrice WaterfallDelta P / BU-Ops handshake
Module 2

Price Setting

Do not apply the same increase everywhere; learn how to differentiate by cost, pricing power, value and product price sensitivity.

Concepts / tools
5-step approachGRTPrice corridorsPrice IndexLPO
Module 3

Price & SPA Governance

Stop giving away margin too easily through discounts/SPAs and make pricing ownership clear.

Concepts / tools
Concession MatrixSPA WidthSPA DepthPricing ownershipApproval / escalation rules
Module 4

Price Animation & Execution

What are the key pricing KPIs, how to read them, how they are interconnected and what to pay attention to during reviews.

Concepts / tools
Delta PricePrice RealizationEMNP / corridorSPA Width / DepthAction plan
